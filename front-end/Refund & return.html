Refund and Return Poilicy

revised on 22/01/2024
Please read the following conditions carefully before accepting them
1. Eligibility for Returns
We want you to have a satisfying shopping experience with M/s Royalties Beauty Cosmetics, and we understand that sometimes returns may be necessary. To be eligible for a return, please make sure to meet the following conditions:
  • The item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • It must be in its original packaging.
  • You must initiate the return process within 7 days from the date of delivered.
  • Proof of purchase, such as a receipt or order number , is required.
2. Return Process
To initiate a return, please follow these steps:
  • Contact our customer support team at royaltiesbcosmetics@gmail.com or 91+8527999563 to request a return. Provide your order number, details of the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason for the return.
  • Our team will review your request and provide you with instructions on how to proceed.
  • Pack the item securely, ensuring it is in its original packaging, and include a copy of the original receipt or order number.
  • Ship the item to the address provided by our customer support team. You will be responsible for the return shipping costs.
3. Refund

Once we receive your returned item and inspect it, we will notify you of the status of your refund. If the return is approved, a refund will be processed to your original payment method within 15 business days.

4. Exchange

If you wish to exchange an item, please follow the same return process as mentioned above. Once your return is received and inspected, we will provide instructions on how to proceed with the exchange.

5. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or provide a refund as per your preference.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund and return policy, please don't hesitate to contact us at royaltiesbcosmetics@gmail.com or 91+ 8527999563 . Beyonist reserves the right to amend or update this refund and return policy at any time. Please check our website for the most current version.
