Glutathione + Kojic = the perfect whitening combination

Often used in skin-lightening treatments, glutathione and kojic acid have antioxidant properties as well as melanin suppression. As a strong antioxidant, glutathione can lighten skin by preventing the development of melanin. Melanin is reduced by kojic acid, which encourages a toned complexion and mild exfoliation.
